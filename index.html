About Us

VJRA Technologies is a smart energy technology company focused on automation, transparency, and reliability.

We help businesses, residential properties, and communities manage electricity and EV charging with zero operational complexity and maximum control.

Our solutions eliminate manual billing, reduce revenue losses, and enable real-time monitoring and revenue assurance through intelligent prepaid energy systems.

What We Do

We design, manufacture, and deploy smart prepaid metering systems and EV charging infrastructure that are secure, scalable, and built for real-world environments.

Our systems are engineered to deliver high uptime, remote operability, and a seamless user experience.

All our products and services are fully customizable to meet the operational and technical requirements of each deployment.
